Jekyll is a static site generator. You write notes in Markdown and it converts them to HTML.

## Key concepts

- **Posts** go in `_posts/` and must be named `YYYY-MM-DD-title.md`
- **Front matter** is the block at the top between `---` lines
- **Layouts** define the HTML wrapper around your content
